Rescue Me Georgia Website Redesign: UX/UI Case Study

Overview

We revamped the website for Rescue Me Georgia, a local non-profit animal rescue organization. Our primary objectives were to enhance first-time visitors' user experience, make it easier for adopters to find information swiftly, and refresh Rescue Me Georgia's logo.

The Challenge

Rescue Me Georgia maintains a website that offers services to adopters and supporters, including donation options, volunteering opportunities, fostering information, and adoption resources. However, some adopters need help navigating the website. Our challenge was to enhance people's understanding of RMG services, improve RMG's reputation, extend its positive influence in society, and boost contributions for donations and volunteering through the website.
